About CAT Reloaded?

CAT a non-profit foundation focused on. Computer Science and IT specially Open Source technologies. CAT's primary goal was to create an IT community in Mansoura University that is dynamic, innovative and highly scalable for the undergraduate computer engineering/science students, that would help them achieve a good level of technical proficiency through projects and seminars.
